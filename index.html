AGENDA OF THE CLUB


• The motive of the club is to gain and share knowledge among ourselves and to keep the pace of our learning growth.

• The senior member of the team must be informed of all the tasks/activities performed by any club member.

• None of the members can impose any decision without concerning the respective senior team member.

• The monthly work agenda must be released by the team in the 1st week of the month with a proper flow of activities/tasks, keeping in view that no two or more events collide in the given date/time.

• If any problem is faced by an individual regarding any issue in the club, then he/she must contact the club's Faculty or President.

• The respective team should reply to the respective queries asked by the student.
E.g.: A student wants to clear the doubt of an MCQ, in that case, an MCQ TEAM's member is supposed to answer the student.

• Equal work distribution must be done, and if in any case, one fails to fulfill their part,he/she must inform to his/her respective senior member with valid reasoning for non-adherance and also will designate any of the following group members to do the task on his/her behalf.

• Any conflicting statement or activity should be avoided and in case found, serious action will be taken.

• According to the abilities of the student, the team will be allotted to them, after allotment no more further changes would be considered.

• Try not to discuss offtopic in any of the groups.
• If any member found not attending the club's meeting, should convey the reason.

RULES AND REGULATION


• MANAGEMENT TEAM

This team is responsible for maintaining the decorum and handling the sub-team accordingly.
Each member will be given equal rights and opportunities.
The management team can contact any sub-team if found not working properly.
This team can only pass the suggestions to the sub-team, the final decision will be done by the respective senior team member.

• MCQ TEAM

The question must be related only to coding and aptitude/reasoning that boosts the student's thinking ability.
If any changes needed, further will be reported.
The questions must not be from any of the online media, and if found, serious action will be there.
The MCQ that will be posted should help everyone irrespective of their year.
If a topic is started (e.g. Machine Learning) then all the questions must be from that topic only for the entire month and on the other hand, question difficulty must be raised from basic to advanced level.
As discussed earlier, the timing for posting must follow the same as directed by team's senior.

• INCISIVE TEAM

If there is a new bunch of students, then the team must handle it within a week.
Question framing and checking should be done by this team for the entrance purpose.
Self-compassed questions are only allowed and the set of questions must be changed every semester.
If the checking is done biasedly, then strict action would be taken against him/her.

• PROJECT TEAM

The project questions must be framed in the view that the majority of them can attend the project and the level must be increased linearly.
Semester wise projects will be uploaded.
Senior members should take care that each student of the team is contributing at least 5 questions per semester.
Questions of the project must be updated within the 1st week of every new semester.
Evaluation and announcement of projects must be done within the semester.

• CHALLENGE TEAM

This team will be responsible for preparing challenges based on coding. This team should come up with new ideas regarding challenges, and this will be conducted every month once or twice if required.
There must be variance in the coding challenge, and try not to repeat the same challenge event in the upcoming future.
Each member of the team must contribute 10% of the challenge.
If the coding event date is announced, then the questions must be prepared 1 week earlier. No last moment rush will be accepted.
Self-composed questions are only required.
If the challenge is once declared then no further changes will take place.

• EVENT TEAM

There must be a single event every month.
All the material regarding the event must be provided to the event team a week before to the Design Team.
If a date is finalized, no further delay of the event should take place.

• DESIGN TEAM

The design team should work on uploading the poster timely, and it should be done twice a day and one should be from coding aspect and other should be from the fun fact or knowledge fact field.
Every semester they should come up with new ideas and implement them accordingly.
Each member should be divided with equal work distribution, and if fails to do so, then the individual should be removed from the club by the senior team member.
The design team will be responsible for making the certificates of the event hosted by the club.
Certificate Design must be done before ending of the event.
Must also come up with new innovative ideas to promote the social media platforms.
